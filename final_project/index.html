Welcome to The Cookbook

Located on the edge of downtown Litteltown, The Cookbook is a small restaurant full of big surprises. All of our dishes are inspired by recipes from Grandma Sally's old cookbook. When the cookbook was recovered in Uncle John's attic, we knew that we had to share with everyone the love and joy of delicious homemade food.
