Summary

The term "spider monkey" actually refers to a large subset of monkeys (order Primates) under the genus Ateles. Essentially all known species and subfamilies of spider monkeys are endangered however, as large swaths of the Amazon Rainforest fall due to a combination of climate change and human deforestation.

Some common species of spider monkeys include:

  • Black-faced black spider monkeys (also known as Peruvian spider monkeys)
  • Brown-headed spider monkeys
  • Geoffroy's spider monkeys
  • Red-faced spider monkeys
  • Brown spider monkeys
  • White-faced spider monkeys
